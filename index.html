About Teepz

Teepz is a game-changing cashless payment platform built specifically for young adults in pre-tertiary boarding schools. Say goodbye to the hassle and delays of sending cash through unreliable channels. With Teepz, parents can send money securely and instantly to their wards — all while maintaining full transparency and control.

Who Teepz is for:

Parents – looking for a reliable, trackable way to support their children
School systems – seeking a streamlined, cash-free campus environment
